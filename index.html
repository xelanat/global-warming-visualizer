Global Warming Visualizer

Berkeley Earth produces a monthly global temperature analysis (e.g. October 2025 Temperature Update), and this visualizer enables a sequential, interactive view of the data over time.

We're only displaying Spatial Variation relative to 1951-1980 average at this time.
